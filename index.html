The story

It all started with my own startup. That's where I learned what entrepreneurship really means.

From there, I dove into the startup ecosystem — working across EdTech, E-Mobility, and more.

Somewhere in between, I took an unexpected turn and served with the Government of India as a Station Master in the Railways.

Every chapter taught me something — how to align teams, scale fast, and persevere when it gets hard.

Today, I'm using those skills to shape the future of learning in India, at PhysicsWallah — building products that matter, feature by feature, release by release.
